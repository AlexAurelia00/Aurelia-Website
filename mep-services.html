What We Offer

MEP (Mechanical, Electrical & Plumbing) systems are the invisible backbone of every building. At Aurelia Designs, we specialize in designing integrated MEP systems that are efficient, scalable, and future-proof. From residential homes to commercial offices, our MEP engineering ensures every system works seamlessly with the others.

We don't just draw plans—we engineer solutions. Energy efficiency, water conservation, intelligent wiring, and optimal HVAC design are built into every project. Our coordination with architectural and structural teams ensures zero conflicts and smooth execution.

Our MEP Design Process

1

Requirement Assessment

We analyze building size, occupancy, climate, regulations, and specific requirements to size systems appropriately.

2

Load Calculations

We calculate electrical, cooling, heating, water supply, and drainage loads to right-size all systems.

3

System Design

Detailed design of electrical, plumbing, and HVAC systems with all specifications, equipment lists, and routing plans.

4

3D BIM Coordination

All MEP systems modeled in 3D and coordinated with architecture and structure. Clashes identified and resolved.

5

Construction Documents & Commissioning

Final drawings and specifications for construction. On-site supervision and commissioning of all systems.
